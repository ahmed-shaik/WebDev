About Us

The Lost and Found Portal is your one-stop solution for reuniting lost items with their rightful owners. Whether you've lost something valuable or found an item that needs to be returned, we are here to help.

Our Features

  • Lost Item Listings: Post details about your lost items and find matches.
  • Found Item Submissions: Help someone reclaim their lost belongings.
  • Advanced Search: Filter and search items by keywords, location, and date.
  • Verification Process: Ensure the rightful owner is reunited with their belongings.

How It Works

  1. Browse or search for lost and found items using our platform.
  2. Submit details of items you've lost or found.
  3. Get notified of potential matches through our system.
  4. Verify ownership and reconnect with your belongings.
